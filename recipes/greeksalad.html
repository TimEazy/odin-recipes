Fresh from the garden Greek Salad

A super fresh recipe that is a staple in the mediterranean diet

This dish is a summer classic because that's the time of the year that most of these vegetables are sold cheap. Cut everything quite large and rough and don't buy cheap oil or feta

Ingredients

  • 1 green capsicum
  • Punnet of cherry tomatos
  • Half a red onion
  • A handful of kalamata olives
  • Cucumber
  • Half a block of feta
  • Red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup of decent olive oil

Method

  1. Cut all the vegetables large and rough and mix together in a bowl
  2. Add the olive oil and a splash of red wine vinegar and incorporate
  3. Add the feta and top with some fresh italian parsley

Serve a big bowl as it's own dish or serve it as a side with some grilled lamb. This dish is simple but delicious.
